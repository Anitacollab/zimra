Why Choose Us

Comprehensive and sustainable products

We offer a range of high-quality products including eggs, broilers, leather goods, manure, and water treatment. Enjoy the convenience of sourcing everything you need from a single, eco-friendly provider.

Top-Notch Quality

Our eggs and broilers are fresh and nutritious, our leather is durable and stylish, and our water treatment services are efficient. We ensure premium quality across all our offerings.

Experienced and Innovative

With years of expertise, we use advanced methods and innovative practices to deliver superior products and services. Trust us for the latest in agricultural and environmental solutions.

CORPORATE INFORMATION

Zimra Integrated Farms Limited with RC No. 1376811 specializes in liquid product processing and agribusiness, including animal feed production, chicken meat and egg production, hygienic water solutions and eco-friendly initiatives. We collaborate with skilled and experience foreign technical partners to deliver comprehensive agro-input supplies. The poultry industry is a significant sector in agriculture, with a growing demand for both eggs and meat due to rising health awareness and population growth. We have extensive experience in poultry farming and agricultural management..
